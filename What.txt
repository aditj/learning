====== What ======
Created Tuesday 19 May 2020

Learning is getting to know things. It’s  A “thing” may very well be anything, discrete or continuous. For the purpose of this article, I’ll be calling the smallest unit of learning to be a ‘lesson’. That is to say that a lesson can’t be further subdivided into more lessons. Note that a combination of lessons can be learned together as a whole.

This lays the basic groundwork required and the unnecessary jargon for this seemingly simple and universal process. Hopefully, by the end of this article, it would seem a little bit less useless.
Now let’s explore the steps involved in learning one at a time.
